    About

    The Golden Dock is a fine-dining restaurant nestled by the lakeside, known for its serene ambience and timeless flavors. Established in 2015, we bring passion to every plate.

    Our Mission

    To serve delicious food in a peaceful lakeside setting, combining traditional flavors with a modern twist.

    Meet Our Chef

    Chef Rohan Kapoor brings 20+ years of culinary experience from around the globe. His passion for fusion cuisine is at the heart of every dish we serve.

    Why Dine With Us?
